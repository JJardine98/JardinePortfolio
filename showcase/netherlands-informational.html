Netherlands Informational Website

A comprehensive digital guide to the Netherlands, featuring interactive historical timelines, cultural insights, culinary experiences, and tourist destinations. This educational platform brings Dutch heritage and modern life to a global audience.

Project Highlights

  • Interactive historical timeline with animated transitions, showcasing major events from Dutch Golden Age to modern era
  • Curated collection of authentic Dutch recipes and food culture, from stroopwafels to contemporary cuisine
  • Visual exploration of iconic destinations including Amsterdam canals, windmills, and tulip fields
  • Detailed guide to Dutch celebrations including King's Day, Sinterklaas, and traditional festivals
  • Data visualization dashboard featuring demographics, economy, and quality of life statistics
  • Responsive design with custom animations and smooth scrolling interactions
  • Educational sections with engaging infographics and interactive learning modules
